Our mission is to save earth, one receipt at a time.

What we do

We are a Toronto based start-up that is here to re-invent the way consumers receive receipts.

We specialize in the research and development of technology that will transition receiving all paper receipts to digital devices instead. Our platform is designed to enable and empower both the retailers and the shopper.

For Retailers

We'll help businesses directly save trees by convert paper receipts into digital receipts. At the same time, we'll help analyze receipt data and help retailers make customer specific promotions.


Our mission is to help retailers build meaningful relationships with consumers and grow their business ethically.
